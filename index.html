Scope and Services Description

Group 107 plans to implement Scope of Work which includes the DevOps Services on the Bank of hours cooperation model.
A detailed description of the tasks with an approximate estimate of the time required to complete them should be attached as an appendix to this proposal if applicable. The minimum number of hours to initiate work is 20 hours.
The hours actually spent on performing the tasks will be indicated in an additional report that will be sent to the Client after the completion of these tasks.
Significant deviations in time should be agreed with the Client in advance.

SLA

All requests shall only be accepted during working days and hours, which are defined as Monday to Friday, 9:00 to 19:00, except for previously announced national holidays, which will be communicated to the client in advance. The list of national holidays shall be shared with the client by the Group 107 Project Manager.
All requests shall be acknowledged and assigned a priority level by the Group 107 Project Manager within one business day of receipt.

The priority levels shall be defined as follows:

  • Critical: website down or malfunctioning
  • High: Issues that impact the functionality of the project or the user experience shall be addressed within 24 hours of request submission.
  • Medium: Issues that affect project performance, but are not critical or high priority, shall be addressed within 36 hours of request submission.
  • Low: Issues that have a minor impact on project performance, such as cosmetic changes or minor modifications, shall be addressed within 48 hours of request submission.

Budget and Payment Terms

Client makes a 100% prepayment based on an approximate estimate of the time required to perform tasks or the minimum number of hours to initiate work. The detailed report with the hours spent and remaining hours from the purchased Bank of hours will be sent to the Client after the approved scope of tasks is completed. Every next purchase of the Bank of hours should precede the work start and include minimum 20 hours.

The following pricing** is applied to the Bank of hours:

  • for the Bank of less than 50 hours, hourly rate is 65 USD.
  • for the Bank of more than 50 hours, hourly rate is 65 USD.
  • every next Bank refill minimum is 20 hours.
